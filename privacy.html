Privacy Policy & Editorial Guidelines

Last Updated: July 7, 2026 • Editorial Standards Division

1. Introduction

HeriTej Pulse is committed to celebrating and documenting the vast cultural heritage of India with the highest standards of journalistic integrity, objectivity, and respect for user privacy. This policy outlines our standards regarding third-party links, syndication feeds, and data protection.

2. Third-Party Linking Guidelines

In order to provide our readers with authoritative context and research validation, HeriTej Pulse frequently links to external cultural organizations, academic journals, and public bodies (such as the Archaeological Survey of India or UNESCO).

"We link to external websites only when they are editorially justified, directly relevant to the narrative, and from trusted sources of cultural or historical record."

Our editorial principles for linking are inspired by global media standards:

  • Editorial Justification: Links are added to clarify, back up, or enrich a story. We do not accept paid or sponsored link placements.
  • Independence: We are not responsible for the content, advertisements, or practices of third-party websites. A link does not constitute an endorsement.
  • Opening Behavior: Outbound links to external organizations open in a new browser tab to maintain user flow on HeriTej Pulse.

3. Feeds & RSS Syndication

HeriTej Pulse supports the syndication of its news headlines and content summaries via RSS/XML feeds for personal, non-commercial use.

  • Non-Commercial Only: Feeds are provided strictly for individuals and non-profit educational institutions. Commercial reuse or syndication onto advertisement-funded portals is prohibited without prior written agreement.
  • Attribution: Any external tool displaying HeriTej Pulse feeds must provide clear, clickable text attribution back to heritej-pulse.com.
  • No Modification: Headings, summaries, and images provided in the feeds must not be modified, cropped, or translated without explicit permission.

4. Privacy and Data Collection

HeriTej Pulse respects your digital privacy. We operate under a "Privacy by Design" model, collecting only the absolute minimum required data to deliver our interactive experiences:

  • Local Browser Storage: We use your browser's local storage (localStorage) to save your bookmarked articles, theme preferences (light/dark mode), and gameplay history (used to adapt difficulty and calculate your Heritage Knowledge IQ).
  • No Centralized Tracking: This data remains entirely on your device and is never uploaded, sold, or shared with third parties or advertising brokers.
  • Analytics: We use basic, privacy-friendly server-level analytics to count page views without tracking user identities or profiles.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions about our editorial standards, external linking policy, or data protection practices, please contact us at:
